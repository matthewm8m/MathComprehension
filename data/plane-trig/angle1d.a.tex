For the angle $\theta = -120^{\circ}$:

$$
\begin{align*}
  \text{smallest positive coterminal angle} &=\ 240^{\circ} \\
  \text{quadrant} &=\ \text{Q}III \\
  \text{reference angle} &=\ 60^{\circ} \\
  \\
  \sin(\theta) &=\ -\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} \\
  \cot(\theta) &=\ +\frac{\sqrt{1}}{\sqrt{3}}
\end{align*}
$$
